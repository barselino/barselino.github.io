Satrio Barselino
about

(b. 2001)


Satrio Barselino is a photographer and artist based in Jakarta, Indonesia. The selected works are heavily informed by a background in painting alongside experience in traditional color darkroom printing.


With extensive knowledge in Color Science focusing on designing image formation pipelines and look development which include spectral calculations and writing complex transformations for custom needs suited for clients ranging from Independent film productions, software developers, to post-production facility.


More color-related writings can be found at blog.
